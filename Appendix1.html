Study 1

You will be randomly assigned to a work scenario. Please read carefully and gradually imagine that you are in the situation described. Try to place yourself in the position of the protagonist and visualize the scenario as vividly as possible.

 

Condition 1: Presence of Algorithmic Management í┴ High Inclusive Leadership

Imagine that you are Wang, a marketing employee at SPARK, an international hotel chain. You are in charge of channel development, online marketing, and offline marketing. Recently, the company has introduced an automatic algorithmic rating system for personnel management, and your daily work is monitored by this system. The system incorporates daily time tracking to monitor your attendance punctuality and working hours, as well as an automatic task allocation module that assigns tasks based on real-time workload data and employeeĘCskill compatibility. Your workload and efficiency are analyzed by algorithms, which calculate the volume of tasks you process and the time required for each task to identify areas where you need to improve efficiency. Performance evaluation is highly detailed: natural language processing is applied to analyze customer service interactions such as call recordings and email communications. This analysis assesses service quality by quantifying response time, problem-solving efficiency, and customer satisfaction to support performance appraisal. A substantial portion of your compensation is determined by the automatic algorithmic rating system.

You work in the marketing department and are supervised by Manager Li. Manager Li is willing to listen to your new ideas and solutions at work, actively seeks opportunities to optimize work processes, and discusses the organizational vision and its implementation with you. You can consult him whenever you encounter work difficulties, as he is consistently present in the team and readily accessible. You can communicate with him in a timely manner when seeking professional advice. In general, Manager Li patiently listens to your thoughts and suggestions, encourages you to discuss problems identified in daily work, and constantly collaborates with you to explore new solutions to emerging issues.
